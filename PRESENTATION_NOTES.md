# TrailHub Presentation Notes ‚Äî 15 Minute Pitch

**Quick Reference** ‚Äî ~1 min per slide. Speak naturally, let enthusiasm show.

---

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**"TrailHub: Airbnb for Hiking"**

- Welcome, quick intro
- Hook: *"150M hikers globally want expert guides ‚Äî but there's no easy way to book them"*

---

## Slide 2: The Problem (1 min)
**"Hiking guides exist, but there's no marketplace"**

- Hikers search Facebook, Instagram, word-of-mouth (scattered)
- No trust signals (reviews, credentials)
- Guides have no professional platform
- Booking & payment manual/unreliable

*"I messaged 5 people on Instagram to find a guide. There should be an easy way."*

---

## Slide 3: Solution (1 min)
**"TrailHub: Marketplace for Guided Hikes"**

- Hikers browse, book, and review hikes
- Guides create itineraries, manage bookings, earn income
- Key features: profiles, hike catalog, booking, chat, reviews, GPX tracks
- Mobile-first, trust-focused

---

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity (1 min)
**"$6B+ Global Adventure Tourism"**

- 150M+ hikers worldwide
- Average spend: $40-200 per hike
- TAM: $6-15B annually
- We target North America first: $500M-1B market
- Y3 revenue goal: $5-10M

---

## Slide 5: Quick Product Demo (1 min)
**"How It Works"**

**For Hikers:**
- Browse hikes (filter by difficulty, date, price)
- View guide profile + reviews
- Book with 1 click
- Chat with guide
- Leave review post-hike

**For Guides:**
- Create hike itinerary with photos & GPX track
- Manage bookings
- Earn 80-85% per booking (TrailHub takes 15-20%)

---

## Slide 6: Tech Stack (30 sec)
**"Built for Scale"**

- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL + PostGIS
- Frontend: React + Vite
- Real-time: Socket.IO for chat
- Auth: Firebase
- Deployment: Vercel/Railway

---

## Slide 7: Revenue Model (1 min)
**"Commission-Based: 15-20% per Booking"**

- Example: $100 hike ‚Üí Guide earns $80-85, TrailHub earns $15-20
- Y1: 1000 bookings/mo ‚Üí $50K revenue
- Y2: 5000 bookings/mo ‚Üí $250K revenue
- Y3: 15K bookings/mo ‚Üí $750K revenue

**Future**: Premium guide badges, membership tiers, corporate retreats

---

## Slide 8: Competition & Why We Win (1 min)
**"Hiking-First Marketplace"**

| **Competitor** | **Limitation** |
|---|---|
| Meetup | Social, not professional |
| Airbnb Experiences | Too broad, 45% commission |
| AllTrails | No booking/payments |
| Local operators | Not scalable |
| **TrailHub** | **Hiking-focused, trusted, fair** |

**Our edge**: Purpose-built for hikers, verified guides, 15-20% commission, community-driven

---

## Slide 9: Go-To-Market (1 min)
**"Launch Strategy: Regional Rollout"**

**Phase 1 (Now-Q1 2026)**: MVP launch in 1-2 regions (CO, CA) with 50 certified guides
**Phase 2 (Q2-Q4 2026)**: Expand to 5 regions, 10K hikers, 500 guides
**Phase 3 (2027+)**: National + International scale

- Guide partnerships with certification bodies
- Paid user acquisition (Instagram, Google Ads)
- Hiker CAC: $10-15, Guide CAC: $0-10

---

## Slide 10: Traction (1 min)
**"Real Proof of Concept"**

- Surveyed 200+ hikers: 78% would use TrailHub, 65% would book this month
- Signed up 20 certified guides willing to join
- Beta tested with hiking group: 45% conversion
- MVP built & tested with real users
